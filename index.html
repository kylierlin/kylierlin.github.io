KYLIE R. LIN

Human-Centered Computing PhD Student

Georgia Institute of Technology

Hi, I’m Kylie! I am a Human-Centered Computing PhD student at Georgia Tech. I work with the GT Visualization Lab and am advised by Cindy Xiong Bearfield.

At the intersection of human cognition and visualization design in Information Visualization, I study how we can help people understand data and make informed decisions. My work focuses on how to use data visualization as a medium to promote critical thinking, engagement, and calibrated confidence judgments.

In the past, I have had the pleasure of collaborating with researchers at Tableau, Adobe, and various research labs at Northwestern University—the Reading Comprehension Lab, Visual Thinking Lab, and Social Media Lab. I am also passionate about User Experience research and have worked in Automotive UX Research at SiriusXM.
