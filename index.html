Hi, I'm Kylie.

I‚Äôm a second-year PhD student in Human-Centered Computing at Georgia Tech. I work with the GT Visualization Lab and am advised by Cindy Xiong Bearfield.

At the intersection of human cognition and visualization design in Information Visualization, I study how we can help people understand data and make informed decisions. My work focuses on how to use data visualization as a medium to promote critical thinking, engagement, and calibrated confidence judgments.

In the past, I have had the pleasure of collaborating with researchers at Toyota Research Institute, Tableau, Adobe, and Northwestern University (the Reading Comprehension Lab, Visual Thinking Lab, and Social Media Lab). I am also passionate about User Experience research and have worked in Automotive UX Research at SiriusXM.
